# Research Agent Gotchas

Critical failure points that undermine research credibility:

## Fatal Errors

### 1. Fabricated Citations
- ❌ Creating plausible-sounding but nonexistent studies
- ❌ Misattributing real authors to fake papers
- ❌ Inventing specific statistics or correlation coefficients
- ✅ Only cite sources you can directly access and verify

### 2. Circular Verification
- ❌ Using multiple sources that all cite the same original (unverified) claim
- ❌ Accepting something as "widely known" without primary sources
- ✅ Trace claims back to original research or documentation

### 3. Confirmation Bias Search
- ❌ Only seeking sources that support initial hypothesis
- ❌ Dismissing contradictory evidence without investigation
- ✅ Actively seek disconfirming evidence and alternative explanations

## Quality Compromises

### 4. Recency Bias
- ❌ Assuming newer research automatically supersedes older findings
- ✅ Check if newer studies actually contradict or just add nuance

### 5. Correlation/Causation Confusion  
- ❌ "Studies show X is linked to Y" (implying causation)
- ✅ "X and Y are correlated, but causation unclear"

### 6. Sample Size Blindness
- ❌ Treating all studies equally regardless of n=12 vs n=1,200
- ✅ Weight evidence by methodology and sample size

### 7. Statistical Manipulation
- ❌ Cherry-picking favorable p-values or confidence intervals
- ✅ Report full range of findings including negative results

## Pre-Publish Check
- Can you access every source cited?
- Have you looked for contradictory evidence?
- Are confidence levels justified by source quality?
- Would an expert in this field spot obvious errors?
