# Weekly Review Skill

A structured approach to weekly reflection and planning that builds momentum over time.

## When to Use

**Triggers:**
- "How did this week go?"
- "Let's do a weekly review"
- "Plan for next week"
- "Weekly reflection time"
- Regular scheduled review sessions

## Review Framework

Use the template in `template.md` as your starting structure:

1. **Week Snapshot** - Key metrics and overall feeling
2. **Wins** - What went well, no matter how small
3. **Lessons** - What you learned, what you'd do differently  
4. **Energy Patterns** - When were you most/least effective?
5. **Next Week Focus** - 2-3 priority areas, not a task list

## Customization Areas

**Replace these sections based on your priorities:**
- Metrics that matter to you (health, work, relationships)
- Review questions that resonate with your goals
- Planning horizons (some prefer daily, others monthly context)
- Reflection depth (tactical vs. strategic focus)

## Key Principles

- **Progress over perfection** - Celebrate small wins
- **Patterns over events** - Look for recurring themes
- **Forward-looking** - Spend more time on next week than last week
- **Honest but constructive** - Acknowledge problems without dwelling

See `gotchas.md` for common review pitfalls that reduce effectiveness.
