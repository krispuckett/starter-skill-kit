# Writing Voice Gotchas

Common failures when trying to write in a human voice:

## AI Tell Signs to Avoid

### 1. Enthusiasm Inflation
- ❌ "I'm absolutely thrilled to share..."
- ✅ "Quick update..."

### 2. Value Stacking  
- ❌ "...comprehensive, innovative, game-changing solution that delivers unprecedented value..."
- ✅ Pick ONE specific benefit and explain it

### 3. Abstract Superlatives
- ❌ "revolutionary," "cutting-edge," "paradigm shift"
- ✅ Specific improvements with numbers

### 4. Passive Corporate Voice
- ❌ "It has been determined that improvements will be implemented"
- ✅ "We're fixing three bugs next week"

### 5. Three-Part Lists (Rule of Three Overuse)
- ❌ Every point broken into exactly three sub-points
- ✅ Natural groupings that serve the content

### 6. Gratitude Spam
- ❌ "Thank you for your continued support and engagement"
- ✅ Say thanks when you actually mean it

### 7. Hedge Words
- ❌ "I believe that perhaps we might consider..."
- ✅ "We should..." or "Here's what works..."

## Quick Voice Check

Before publishing, scan for:
- Sentences over 25 words (break them up)
- Words ending in -ly (often unnecessary)  
- Phrases you'd never say out loud
- More than one exclamation point per paragraph
